Pre-competition tasks

Robot calibration
0. Confirm drivetrain width (24"?)
1. Tune driver controls via preferences: boost, sensitivity, turn correction, rate change limiting
2. Tune tank drive for path driving
	- Uncomment in arcade drive command to use tank drive
	- Tune variables in normalizedTankDrive in Drivetrain until robot drives forward straight with prefs, then remove prefs

Paths
1. Draw side switch, cross baseline, side scales in OnShape
2. Add waypoints to spreadsheet and Waypoints in code
